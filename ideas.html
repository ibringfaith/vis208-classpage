Three Ideas or Starting Points for My "Lightweight Format"

1. Memorable Movie Scene

Guide:

  • Take a photo of something that represents the movie to you
  • Write the movie name
  • Describe the scene in a memorable quote from the movie

Examples:


  • Legally Blonde

    “Remember that first impressions are not always correct. You must always have faith in people. And most importantly, you must always have faith in yourself.”


  • How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

    "Andie: Oh, no. Our love fern. It's dead. Ben: No, it's just sleeping. Andie: You let it die. Are you going to let us die?"


  • Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

    "Sometimes I let matches burn down to my fingertips just to feel something, anything."

2. Diary Entry

Guide:

  • Write the current date
  • Take a photo at some point during your day
  • Write a short caption of the day's events and what you were feeling

Examples:

  • 2/14/2024 Wednesday


    I baked sugar cookies and decorated them. I felt happy that I did not burn them. And I'm so glad they actually taste great :D

  • 6/20/2019 Sunday


    I visited my cousin today. Look how much Tigress has grown! Kinda sad that I missed out on so much, but I plan to visit more often :)

  • 8/6/2014 Wednesday


    I woke up, went to the beach, and spent the entire day either in the water or in the sand. About to head to dinner now. Feeling a lot of joy right now. I love summer!!!

3. Wishlist

Guide:

  • When you want to purchase something, take a picture of the item and add it here
  • Write the day that you wanted to make the purchase and why you want to purchase it
  • Come back a month later to see if it's still something you want

Examples:

  • 02/15 It's so pretty, will lighten up the parlor, and add some much needed color!

    03/15 What was I thinking...

  • 02/15 My bedroom walls need to be redone. I just need to decide on a color.

    03/15 Still deciding... will revisit in a month!

  • 02/15 I accidently left my old ones at the pool and now I cannot find them :( They're really comfy.

    03/15 Buying now!
